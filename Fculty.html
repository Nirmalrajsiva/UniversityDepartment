DEPARTMENT OF NETWORK SYSTEM AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

DETAIL INFORMATION

HISTORY

This Department was established in the year 2014. Dr. Ananthi from Central Instrumentation and Service Laboratory was working as Head in-charge of the Department from 2014 -2018. From July 2018 to December 2020 Prof. Dr. G. Anbalagan of Nuclear Physics Department was working as Head in-charge of the Department.

STATUS

This Department is currently engaged in teaching and research activities in different areas of Networking Systems and Information Technology. Duing the academic year 2019-20, a two year M.Sc. program in Information Techonolgy was started.
